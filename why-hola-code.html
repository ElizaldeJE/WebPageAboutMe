Why HolaCode?

I found out about HolaCode one day that I was doing overtime (extra hours) during primetime when I went out to take a break and get some food. I was just getting outside when I stumbled upon a friend that was coming back from his lunch and told that a dude from here was just there giving out these little flyers and explaining a little bit about the program. He told me what he understood and gave me one of the flyers, I thought about it for a moment then that same day I called the number that was on the paper which turned out to be Leni, we talked for a bit and she explained with more details what it was all about. I did not know what to expect from the informative session the following day, once I got there it all seemed like a very small program. Now when the presentation started and the HolaTeam started giving all the details about the whole program I was very excited. I left the session sure that I was going to join and do my best to make sure that I follow with the program and get the most out of it since this is a once in a life time opportunity.

The reason why I decided to take this challenge upon on is because this is an upgrade to my life, not only intellectually but also professionally wise. Just a few days before I was looking into the university IPN to do my carrer there for mechatronics. But with this there's no doubt I will succeed. I am extremely grateful to everyone who helped pull this proyect together and make it happen.

Work History

Fighting

I got into making money from fighting profesionally wise, after high school I tried to get into med school but it did not happen so I started working out to invest my time in something. So I joined a gym near my house, since I had done wrestling preveiously in high school for a couple of years I decided to take on MMA which was taught in the same gym. Turns out I was pretty good at it, then one day the box professor asked me if I wished to get in the ring for a couple of rounds to get a hang of it, from that moment on everything changed. I joined boxing, BJJ, Muay Thai (which I ended up chosing this one as my main style) and Capoeira. It became like a job since I was in there training everyday from Monday to Saturday at least 9 hours. Once I had the skills necessary I requested my first fight, I was just doing it for the love of the sport and adrenaline. After a couple of fights I got sponsored by BoneBreakers, signed a contract with them and for everyfight I would get paid. Since I love this so much I also started giving classes to younger kids. I do still fight every once in a while but not in the same level as I used to, I do plan on going back to the ring but first I need to get my life in order so then I can at least do this as a hobbie, get a fight in there every other month at least.
